Tangram Game

Rebuild the square by moving, rotating, and flipping the 7 pieces.

  • Drag with left mouse to move a piece
  • Mouse wheel rotates the selected piece freely
  • Press F to flip the selected piece
  • Press R to reset to the solved layout
  • Press S to shuffle

Solve the puzzle without a visible placement board. Use “Show solution” only if you want to give up.

Arrange all pieces into the square.

Note: This uses the standard 7-piece tangram set: 2 large triangles, 1 medium triangle, 2 small triangles, 1 square, and 1 parallelogram.

Self-tests: Basic geometry tests run automatically on load.
